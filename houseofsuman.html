The House of Suman - Clarin, Misamis Occidental

The House of Suman in Clarin, Misamis Occidental, is a cultural and culinary landmark known for its delicious and authentic suman, a traditional Filipino rice cake. Located in the heart of Clarin, it has become a popular destination for food lovers and tourists who want to taste this local delicacy.

Key Features of The House of Suman:

Traditional Cooking Methods: The suman served at this iconic place is prepared using traditional methods passed down through generations. The rich flavors and soft texture make it one of the best in the region.

Local Favorite: Known for being a favorite snack and dessert in the area, the suman from the House of Suman is often paired with hot chocolate or coffee, making it the perfect treat for both locals and visitors alike.

Family-Owned Business: The House of Suman is a family-run establishment, and the owners take great pride in their craft, serving customers with the same passion and care as their ancestors did.

Accessible Location: Located in the town of Clarin, the House of Suman is easy to find, and many locals recommend stopping by to get a taste of this delicious treat when visiting the area.

Perfect for Souvenir Shopping: Aside from enjoying the suman, visitors can also purchase the delicacies to take home as souvenirs, making it a great stop for those wanting to bring a taste of Clarin with them.
